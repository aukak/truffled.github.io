What happened?

On July 11, 2023, we at Mercury Workshop received a notice from Google, informing us to take down both the downloads for the SH1MMER bins and the website builder.

Not wanting to deal with the legality of it all, we had no choice but to comply with what they requested from us, and the builder & bins have been taken offline.

This doesn't mark the end of things, but it does mean that we can no longer provide a place for users to download SH1MMER bins nor build a injected shim.

For more information, check the Discord Server.
